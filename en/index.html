GeoGuessr

GeoGuessr is a game based on Google Street View, a service allowing to walk in the streets of the different countries of the world.

The goal: finding in a fixed amount of time our position, as precisely as possible.

Between a video game and a knowledge game, players use the different signs next to the road, landscapes or architecture to narrow down the place where they are.

Our community

An official association since 2023 and created in 2020, La Ligue is a meeting space around geography and games, as well as a way to meet fellow geography fans!

La Ligue Geoguessr gathers a wide spectrum of talented people, with players developing their ability to learn and execution daily.

All those competitions could not exist without the investment and expertise of a lot of people in development, spreadsheets, geomatics or graphics.
